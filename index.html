About Us

ITD is a non-profit skills development organisation founded by young people from Ivory Park and Tembisa to address youth education and unemployment challenges.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

A reality where individuals in townships and rural areas rise to their full developmental potential.

Mission

Provide transformative educational, training and economic opportunities through holistic youth development programmes.

Values

  • Ubuntu
  • Collaboration
  • Resourcefulness
  • Accountability
  • Creativity

Academic support, vocational training, career guidance and poverty alleviation initiatives.
